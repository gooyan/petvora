Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PetVora ("PetVora", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the PetVora mobile application, wearable devices, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). By using the Services, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account information — name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Pet profile — your pet's name, type, breed, age, weight, and similar details you choose to add.
  • Support communications — information you share when you contact us.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Location data — GPS and approximate location of the PetVora device, used to provide tracking, location history, and safe-zone alerts.
  • Activity & health signals — motion, activity, rest, and wellbeing metrics measured by the device sensors.
  • Device & usage data — device identifiers, app version, operating system, battery status, connectivity status, diagnostics, and crash logs used to keep the Services reliable.
PetVora does not sell your personal information, and we do not use your pet's location or health data for advertising.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core features — live location, safe-zone alerts, activity tracking, and health monitoring.
  • Sync your data across your account and devices.
  • Send notifications you have enabled, such as boundary or safety alerts.
  • Operate, maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the Services.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Location Data

Location is essential to PetVora's core purpose. We collect location from the PetVora device to show you where your pet is and to trigger safe-zone alerts. You can manage app location permissions at any time through your device settings, though disabling them will limit tracking features.

4. How We Share Information

We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who host our infrastructure, deliver notifications, or provide analytics, acting on our instructions and bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements — when required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.
  • Safety — to protect the rights, property, or safety of PetVora, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.
  • With your consent — when you direct us to share information.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Section 7).

6. Data Security

We use encryption in transit, access controls, and other industry-standard safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work continuously to protect your data and respond promptly to any incidents.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@petvora.com. You can also:

  • Edit your account and pet profile in the app.
  • Manage location and notification permissions in your device settings.
  • Request account deletion by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

The Services are intended for adults and are not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. Where required, we put appropriate safeguards in place for cross-border transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice through the app.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@petvora.com
  • Support: support@petvora.com
  • Web: petvora.com
