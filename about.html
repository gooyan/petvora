About PetVora

Smart wearables for the pets you love

PetVora is a smart wearable brand for dogs and cats. We build lightweight, connected collars and a companion mobile app that bring together four things every pet parent cares about: where your pet is, whether they're safe, how active they are, and how they're feeling.

Our mission is simple: give every pet parent the confidence that comes from truly understanding their companion — and the early warning that can make all the difference.

What we make

The PetVora collar pairs with the PetVora app to deliver:

  • Location — real-time GPS tracking and location history.
  • Safety — customizable safe zones with instant boundary alerts.
  • Activity — daily steps, active minutes, rest, and goal tracking.
  • Health — continuous monitoring of key wellbeing signals, with trends over time.

Why we built it

Pets can't tell us when something is wrong, and they can't call home when they're lost. PetVora was created to close that gap — turning continuous, gentle monitoring into clear, actionable insight so that small problems get noticed early and lost pets get found fast.

Privacy first

Your pet's data — and your data — belong to you. We collect only what's needed to make PetVora work, we're transparent about how it's used, and we never sell personal information. Read the full details in our Privacy Policy.
